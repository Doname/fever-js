Help, Credits and Copyrights ©

Support, bug reports et. al.

This app is still a work in progress. It works quite well, but has not been fully tested for security aspects and such cases.

If you encounter bugs, miss features or have a comment about fever-js, go to fever-js on github.com and post your questions there. If you want to help out on features, bugs or documentation, you are welcome to contribute to fever-js on github.com in any way you find fitting. If you want to make your own adjustments to fever-js I suggest to fork it on githib. That way your features my flow back into fever-js.

News, comments and announcements about fever-js are posted to my webpage.

You like social networks? If so, you can like fever-js and tell your friends about it on Facebook, Google+, LinkedIn or Xing. Have question about fever-js? You can send me a tweet to @simplyhg on twitter (I'm not too active there, but I receive and try to answer messages and tweets - maybe I even get more active there, who knows).
